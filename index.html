About Us

Founded in Tokyo, Japan, **JM Camera** is a small, dedicated company passionate about the art of analog photography. We believe that classic film cameras offer a unique tactile experience and produce images with unparalleled character, especially when they embody the renowned **Japan quality**.

Our mission is to give these beautiful machines a second life. We carefully select, inspect, clean, and test each camera to ensure it meets our high standards before it reaches your hands. This dedication to quality is why we've achieved a **100% positive evaluation from our customers on eBay**. From iconic SLRs to charming rangefinders, we strive to offer a diverse collection for photographers of all levels.

By selling directly, we aim to provide a more personal and cost-effective experience for our international clients, fostering a community of film photography enthusiasts who appreciate genuine Japan quality.
